Hackers Range (*Student side)

Industry:

Cybersecurity Training Platform

Product overview:

Hackers Range is the ultimate cybersecurity training and up-skilling platform, combining real-world simulations with gamification to make practical, hands- on expertise accessible while keeping the experience educational, engaging, competitive, and fun. HackersRange is a hands-on cybersecurity training and simulation platform that enables organizations, academies, and vendors to create and deliver interactive cyber labs, gamified training, and structured learning paths in realistic environments. The platform supports onboarding, skill evaluation, and real-world scenario practice through a multi-role system with dedicated admin and student experiences.

Student Environment Screens:

The student side is the execution layer of the platform where learners access and complete the training content created in the LMS.

Courses and games dynamically present tasks, labs, and learning materials based on the structure defined in the admin environment.

The experience is designed to keep users focused on completing challenges while providing supporting learning resources such as videos, PDFs, and lessons when neede
