My Process - How we solve your problems?

A successful project requires deep collaboration between Clinet & Designer.
The problems we solve can be intersection of Design and Code.

Discovery

The first step is information gathering.Research intensively client competition. Listeing to understand clinet needs in order to determine exactly what they need. This involves a questionnaire, design audit, and technical review. We create a plan for your new Project that you need,/website,logo etc/ based on your specific needs.

Design

We start doing design based on our plan from discovery. Great design is a combination of look and feel. It dictates the first impression visitors will have of your business and brand.Example page spreads and layouts are produced. Mockups and proofs can be developed to give you an idea of what the final product will look like.

Development

We begin to bring the designs to life!We turn the mockups into reality. Concepts are presented and your feedback and comments are gathered. Depending on what your project entails, that could mean developing a functional Website. Once the project has been built,we ensure a successful launch.
