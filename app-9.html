Suite

Industry:

Cybersecurity / Cyber Threat Intelligence Platform/

Product overview:

Suite is a SOC/MDR cybersecurity platform for real-time threat monitoring, alert management, and incident investigation. It centralizes security data into a single analytical workspace where teams can track organizational risk, investigate alerts, manage cases, and analyze suspicious files and assets.

My role:

Led the end-to-end UX/UI design from concept to final design. Collaborated with product managers and security experts to translate complex technical requirements and raw security data into clear dashboards, structured tables, and investigation workflows.

UX challenges:

• Transforming large volumes of raw security data into clear, actionable insights

• Designing for expert users who need speed, accuracy, and high data density

• Creating a scalable layout that supports real-time monitoring and future feature growth

• Establishing visual hierarchy for alert prioritization and risk awareness

Outcome:

• Delivered a complete analytical interface for SOC/MDR workflows

• Enabled faster threat detection and investigation through structured dashboards and tables

• Created a consistent design language for complex security data

• Designed responsive email notification templates for alert events
